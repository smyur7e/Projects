Recent Significant Projects

Illicit Tobacco Control

Client Department of Home Affairs
Duration September 2016 - now

The Integrated Cargo System (ICS) is a email/web interfaced application for managing, profiling, calculating duty on cargo. It handles over 250,000 cargo reports and declartions. Its core consists of over 200 CICS transactions and batch applications that were developed using CA Gen. These system components together profile cargo and estimate duty on various imported goods. My role was to help design and develop the Import Permit sub-system and help enhance the performance of the cargo profiling transactions.
